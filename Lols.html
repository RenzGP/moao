Tourism

Aurora is endowed with a plethora of natural tourism attractions. Its waterfalls, beaches, historic sites, lush green mountains, and blue ocean seas, as well as the verdant country pastures and farmlands, are breathtaking. The majority of these natural wonders have remained untouched. Tourism might thus be viewed as a potentially significant growth business.

Agriculture

Agriculture is Aurora's principal sector, employing over half of the population. Cultivated land covers around 49,991 hectares, or 15% of the province land area.
Rice, coconut, coffee, bananas, root crops, corn, citrus fruits, peanuts, and abaca are among the major crops.

Financial Status

The province's total appropriation is PhP 839, 379, 437.77. As of December 31, 2019, a total of PhP 717,777,094.83 (about 85.51% of total appropriations) had been obligated.
